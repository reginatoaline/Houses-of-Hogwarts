Ravenclaw

Intelligence - Learning - Wisdom - Wit


Colors and Mascot: Blue and Bronze. Eagle.
Founder: Rowena Ravenclaw.
Head of the House: Filius Flitwick
House Ghost: The Grey Lady.
Common Room Location: Ravenclaw Tower, beyond a bronze Eagle shaped knocker that poses a riddle.
Famous Members: Luna Lovegood, Gilderoy Lockhart, Cho Chang, Quirinus Quirrell, Sybill Trelawney, Garrick Ollivander.

slytherin

Ambition - Cunning - Leadership - Resourcefulness


Colors and Mascot: Green and Silver. Serpent.
Founder: Salazar Slytherin
Head of the House: Severus Snape
House Ghost: The Bloody Baron
Common Room Location: In the dungeons, behind an apparently blank stretch of wall.
Famous Members:Tom Riddle, Draco Malfoy, Bellatrix Lestrange, Horace Slughorn, Dolores Umbridge, Lucius Malfoy.

hufflepuff

Hard Work - Patience - Justice - Loyalty


Colors and Mascot: Yellow and Black. Badger.
Founder: Helga Hufflepuff
Head of the House: Pomona Sprout
House Ghost: Fat Friar
Common Room Location: Hufflepuff Basement, near the kitchen.
Famous Members: Nymphadora Tonks, Cedric Diggory, Newton Scamander, Hannah Abbott, Zacharias Smith, Suan Bones.

gryffindor

Courage - Bravery - Nerve - Chivalry

Colors and Mascot: Red and Gold. Lion.
Founder: Godric Griffindor
Head of the House: Minerva McGonagall
House Ghost: Sir Nicholas de Mimsy-Porpigton (Nearly Headless Nick)
Common Room Location: Gryffindor Tower, through the Fat Lady's portrait.
Famous Members: Albus Dumbledore, Harry Potter, Ronald Weasley, Hermione Granger, Rubeus Hagrid, Sirius Black, Remus Lupin, James Potter, Lily Evans, Peter Pettigrew, Ginny Weasley,
